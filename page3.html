Academics and Design

Some of the most uniquely designed academic buildings belong to the most prestigious schools

Columbia University’s Vagelos Education Center

  • This medical center is praised by Architectural Digest as one of the best university buildings in the world. It is composed of nearly all glass and holds modern classrooms and practice labs.


    • Why design?

    Innovatively designed academic buildings are not only fascinating to the eye. They serve as hubs for state-of-the-art technology, often hold historical or political significance, and are found to be stimulating environments for student productivity.
